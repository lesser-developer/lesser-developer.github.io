History

Two generations after the founding of Space Station 0001, Zyra Kallix made a shocking discovery: logs and diaries from the pioneers who first ventured forth from Earth were in danger of being lost.

Dismayed by this discovery, Zyra decided to archive as many logs and diaries from those first ships as possible. She spent two decades sourcing, formatting, and publishing the words of the first humans to leave Earth without hope or intention of return. And when she had finished, she founded Pioneer Books to make those same logs and diaries available to the masses.

Pioneer Books has been a family business since Zyra first opened its doors in 6344. Since then, more diaries and logs have been discovered and preserved for future generations, and can be purchased in formats suitable for all readers.

Through it all, Vos reflects on survival, loyalty, and humanity’s fragile hope of finding a new home among the stars.
